Place your song here as **song.mp3** so music plays inside the site.

The YouTube video (lY5V4hSLWY8) does not allow embedding, so it cannot play in an iframe.
Export or convert the song to MP3 and save it as:

```
frontend/confession/public/music/song.mp3
```

Then run:

```
.\scripts\build_frontend.ps1
```

Until song.mp3 exists, tapping play will open the song on YouTube in a new tab.
